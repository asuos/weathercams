About the cams

All streaming gear was either purchased by members of the ASU Webmaster's Office or pulled from the boneyard of old computers. No state funds have been spent on this project. DIY #ftw!

Want to host a cam? It's easy!

We're always looking for new and exciting places to put cams around campus, we do have some requirements:

  • You should have a clear view of campus with an interesting view.
  • It must be indoors.
  • The location needs to be close to a power outlet and a network port.
  • You must be able to secure the area.

Send us a message through our Facebook page or Email us at webmaster[at]appstate[dot]edu and we'll disscuss getting a cam setup.

Can't see the cam?

A few things you can try:

  • Try using a different browser, we do not support Internet Explorer or old versions of Firefox because of licensing issues with the codec we use to stream. Try using Google Chrome or a newer version of Firefox, let us know if it doesn't work.
  • Check our our Facebook page and see if anyone else is haveing a simular problems. Feel free to post a message and let us know.
